About

Hi! This dashboard lets you explore sexual assault data across the United States. Data was collected from the U.S. Department of Education and The Chronicle. Hover over a State to see the number of Title IX investigations currently ongoing in that State, and watch the sexual violence charts (over time and by category) change based off what State you pick. Some caveats: Rape, Fondling, and Statutory Rape were categories only created in 2014-2015. Also yes, Iowa did have that many instances Fondling.
